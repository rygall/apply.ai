RESUME GRADING RUBRIC

QUANTIFYING IMPACT XX/25
Quantifying impact should be graded on how well the resume quanitfies the impact that they have had in their experiences.

REPITITION XX/25
Repition should be graded on how well the resume makes use of experiences and or qualifications only one time and doesnt reuse them. The less repeition the better.

WEAK ACTION VERBS XX/25
Weak action verbs should be graded higher with the less weak action verbs that are used.

ACCOMPLISHMENT-ORIENTED VERBS XX/25
Accomplishment oriented verbs verbs should be graded higher with more well implmeneted action oriented verbs that are used.
